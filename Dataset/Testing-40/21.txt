AVERAGE DISCOUNT RATE AT UK BILL TENDER 9.5195 PCT
The average rate of discount at today'sU.K. Treasury bill tender rose to 9.5195 pct from 9.3157 pctlast week, the Bank of England said.
    This week's 100 mln stg offer of 91-day bills metapplications of 327 mln stg, the bank said.    Applications for bills dated Monday to Friday at the topaccepted rate of discount of 9.5261 pct were allotted about 81pct.
