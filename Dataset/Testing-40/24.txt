SPAIN RAISES CALL MONEY RATE TO 14.5 PCT
The Bank of Spain said it raised its ratefor overnight call money to 14.5 pct from 14 pct with immediateeffect at today's daily auction for assistance funds.    The move followed comments yesterday by central bankgovernor Mariano Rubio, who said money supply growth was toofast.
    The bank said later that it was leaving its rate forspecial assistance funds from its second window unchanged at 16pct, surprising operators who had expected an increase theretoo. REUTER
