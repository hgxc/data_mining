TEXACO CANADA <TXC> LOWERS CRUDE POSTINGS
Texaco Canada said it lowered thecontract price it will pay for crude oil 64 Canadian cts abarrel, effective today.    The decrease brings the company's posted price for thebenchmark grade, Edmonton/Swann Hills Light Sweet, to 22.26Canadian dlrs a bbl.
    Texaco Canada last changed its crude oil postings on Feb19. Reuter
