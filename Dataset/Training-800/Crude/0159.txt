SAUDIS DROP CONDITION FOR OIL SALE TO BRAZIL
Saudi Arabia has dropped itscondition that Brazil secure international bank guaranteesbefore Saudia Arabia would ship it oil, the state-oil company,Petrobras, said in a statement.    Petrobras said the Saudis will accept Banco do Brasilcredit guarantees.    Petrobras cancelled a 40-mln dlr crude oil purchase fromthe Saudis yesterday after they refused to accept a letter ofcredit from the official Bank of Brazil.
 The Saudis haddemanded that Brazil get credit guarantees from leadinginternational banks.    Petrobras said the Saudis had been advised that if they didnot change their mind by Monday, Petrobras would negotiate thepurchase of oil with other producers.
