USX <X> UNIT TO RAISE MOST CRUDE POSTINGS
USX's subsidiary, Marathon PetroleumCo, said it will raise its contract prices for eleven grades ofcrude oil 50 cts a barrel, effective tomorrow.    The increase brings Marathon's posted price for West TexasIntermediate and West Texas Sour grades to 17.50 dlrs a barrel.
    The Light Louisiana grade was also raised 50 cts to 17.85dlrs a barrel. Reuter
