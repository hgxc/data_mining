MOBIL PLANS TO OPEN OFFICE IN PEKING
Mobil Oil Corp of U.S. Plans to open anoffice in Peking to develop oil exploration opportunities inChina, the China Daily said.     It quoted Mobil president Richard Tucker, currently inPeking, as saying he is optimistic about investment prospectsin China and that Peking will continue to encourage foreignprivate businesses to invest here.     It said Mobil bought 73 mln dlrs of crude oil and oilproducts from China in 1986 and sold it lubricant andfertiliser, but gave no more details.
