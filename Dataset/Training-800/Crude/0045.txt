SOUTHLAND <SLC> UNIT RAISES CRUDE PRICES
Southland Corp's Citgo Petrleum Corpsaid it raised the contract price it will pay for all grades ofcrude oil by one dlr a barrel, effective today.    The increase brings Citgo's posted price for West TexasIntermediate to 17.00 dlrs a bbl.
 West Texas Sour is also nowpriced at 17.00 dlrs/bbl, and Light Louisiana South is postedat 17.35 dlrs/bbl.
    On February 25 Citgo lowered its crude postings 50 cts to1.50 dlrs per bbl, and cut WTI one dlr to 16.00.
