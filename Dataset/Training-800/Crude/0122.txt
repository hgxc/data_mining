POWERINE REFINERY TO RESUME OPERATIONS
<Powerine Oil Co> saidits refinery here will resume operation today, with initialcrude oil runs of 20,000 barrels per day.    Powerine, a privately-held company, said it expects therefinery to become fully operational by May, with crude oilthroughput at 35,000 bpd.    Initial oil products deliveries are scheduled for Monday,Powerine said.
 The refinery had been closed due to a change ofownership, the company said. Reuter
