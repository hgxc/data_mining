IRANIAN OIL MINISTER DUE IN ALGERIA ON FRIDAY
Iranian Oil Minister Gholamreza Aqazadehis expected here on Friday for talks with his Algeriancounterpart Belkacem Nabi, the official Algerian news agencyAPS said today.    Aqazadeh, who will be accompanied by a large delegation,will have talks on bilateral relations in the field of energyand exchange views with Algerian officials on the current worldenergy situation, it said.
