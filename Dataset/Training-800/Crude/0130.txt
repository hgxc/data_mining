PHILLIPS <P> RAISES CRUDE POSTINGS 50 CTS
Phillips Petroleum said it raised thecontract price it will for all grades of crude oil 50 cts abarrel, effective today.    The increase brings Phillip's posted price for the WestTexas Intermediate and West Texas Sour grades to 17.50 dlrs abbl.
    Phillips last changed its crude oil postings on March 4.    The price increase follows similar moves by USX's <X>subsidiary, Marathon oil, and Sun Co <SUN> earlier today. Reuter
