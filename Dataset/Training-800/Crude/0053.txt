DIAMOND SHAMROCK <DIA> RAISES CRUDE OIL POSTINGS
Diamond Shamrock said it raised itsposted prices for all grades of crude oil one dlr a barrel,effective yesterday.    The one dlr increase brings West Texas Intermediate, WTIthe U.S. benchmark crude, to 17.00 dlrs a bbl, the companysaid.
    Diamond Shamrock joined Sun Co, Coastal, Citgo and MurphyOil in raising crude oil posted prices one dlr a barrelyesterday. Reuter
