AMOCO REPORTS SOUTH CHINA SEA OIL FIND
The U.S. <Amoco Petroleum Corp> hasreported an offshore oil find at its Pearl River basinconcession in the South China Sea, the New China News Agencysaid.    It said the Liu Hua 11-1-1 A well produced at around 2,240barrels per day at a depth of 305 metres.    The news agency said Amoco plans to drill a second well inthe area this year, but gave no further details.
