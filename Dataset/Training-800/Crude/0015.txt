PHILADELPHIA PORT CLOSED BY TANKER CRASH
The port of Philadelphia was closedwhen a Cypriot oil tanker, Seapride II, ran aground afterhitting a 200-foot tower supporting power lines across theriver, a Coast Guard spokesman said.    He said there was no oil spill but the ship is lodged onrocks opposite the Hope Creek nuclear power plant in NewJersey.    He said the port would be closed until today when theyhoped to refloat the ship on the high tide.
