PANCANADIAN TO SELL BRITISH INTERESTS
<PanCanadian Petroleum Ltd>said it agreed to sell its working interest in its North Seaproperties and its British unit, Canadian Pacific Oil and Gasof Canada Ltd, to Whitehall Petroleum Ltd, a private Britishcompany.    PanCanadian, 87 pct-owned by Canadian Pacific Ltd <CP>,said it would receive 1.7 mln British pounds cash (3.
5 mlnCanadian dlrs) at closing, expected in two to three months.    It said the deal is subject to approval by regulators andits partners in the properties, which consist of explorationwells. It will also retain a royalty interest in theproperties.
