SUN <SUN> RAISES CRUDE POSTINGS 50 CTS
Sun Co said it raised the contractprice it will pay for crude oil 50 cts a barrel, effectivetoday.    The increase brings Sun's posted price for the West TexasIntermediate and West Texas Sour grades to 17.50 dlrs/bbl.
 TheLight Louisiana Sweet grade was also raised 50 cts to 17.85dlrs/bbl.    Sun Co last changed its crude postings on March 4.
