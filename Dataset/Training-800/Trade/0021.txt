EC APPOINTS NEW TRADE CHIEF
The European Community Commission todayappointed its chief spokesman, Hugo Paemen, as its top officialin charge of multilateral trade negotiations, a Commissionspokesman said.    Paemen, a Belgian official who had previously been chiefaide to former External Affairs Commissioner Etienne Davignon,has been in his post since January 1985.    The spokesman said Paemen will continue as chief spokesmanuntil the retirement on May 1 of Paul Luyten, who is now incharge of EC departments handling negotiations in the worldtrade body GATT, the OECD and other forums.
