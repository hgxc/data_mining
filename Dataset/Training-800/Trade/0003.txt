SWEDEN TO GO AHEAD WITH S. AFRICAN TRADE SANCTIONS
Sweden's ruling Social DemocraticParty gave full power to the government to decree unilateraltrade sanctions against South Africa, Prime Minister IngvarCalrsson said.    Carlsson told a news conference the party decided the fightagainst apartheid took priority over Sweden's traditionalpolicy of only adopting sanctions with the backing of the U.N.Security Council.    The government will decide later what form the tradeboycott will take and when it will come into force.
