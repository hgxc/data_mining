BANK OF JAPAN CALLS FOR LONG-TERM EFFORTS ON TRADE
The short-term effect of foreign exchangerate movements in correcting international trade imbalancesappears to be waning, and long-term efforts are required to cutJapan's chronic dependence on external demand, the Bank ofJapan said in a monthly report.    Japan's trade surplus in nominal terms is likely to remainhigh in the near future, the central bank said.    Fundamental adjustments will be needed as long as Japanhopes to benefit from a better international allocation ofresources and maintain the free trade system, it added.
