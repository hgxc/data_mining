ROTTERDAM SIGNS COOPERATION AGREEMENT WITH TOKYO
The city of Rotterdam today signed anagreement in principle to cooperate with the AOMI CargoDistribution Centre in Tokyo.    Acting Mayor Roel den Dunnen said that cooperation betweenprivate and public entities in the Tokyo and Rotterdam area,and a fruitful exchange of information have a favourableinfluence on the flow of goods and services between the twocountries.    The AOMI Cargo Distribution Center, which will startoperating in October this year, signed a similar agreement withRotterdam's twinned-port of Seattle last September.
