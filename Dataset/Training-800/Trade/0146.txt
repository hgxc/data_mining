INDIA, AUSTRALIA AGREE TO IMPROVE TWO-WAY TRADE
Indian and Australian businessmensigned a memorandum of understanding to boost two-way trade andjoint industrial ventures in both countries, spokesmen of theIndo-Australia Business Council told reporters.    The Indian Council said it hoped India's exports toAustralia would improve in the next few months and reduce thetrade balance which is heavily in Australia's favour.
