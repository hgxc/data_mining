TAIWAN TO BOOST FARM IMPORTS FROM U.S., EUROPE
Taiwan is expected to boost agriculturalimports from the U.S. And Europe in calendar 1987 to helpbalance trade, the Council of Agriculture said. 38 billionin 1985.    Taiwan's surplus with the U.S. Rose to 13.6 billion dlrslast year from 10.
