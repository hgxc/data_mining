BANKERS TRUST <BT> RAISES PRIME LENDING RATE
Bankers Trust Co said it has raised itsprime lending rate to 7-3/4 pct from 7-1/2, effectiveimmediately.    This move is the latest in a series of similar actions byleading U.S. money center banks, including Citibank NA andChase Manhattan Bank NA, over the last 24 hours.    AmeriTrust Corp of Ohio also raised its prime lending rateto 7-3/4 pct from 7-1/2, effective tomorrow.
