CITIBANK FOLLOWS U.K. BASE RATE CUT TO 10.5 PCT
<Citibank NA> said it has cut its Britishbase lending rate to 10.5 pct from 11 pct, with effect fromtomorrow.    This move follows similar cuts by four major Britishclearing banks, led by National Westminster Bank Plc <NWBL.L>.
    Lower rates were signalled by the Bank of England thismorning in an attempt to curb recent upward pressure on thepound, market sources said. REUTER
