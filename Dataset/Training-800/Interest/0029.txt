NATIONAL WESTMINSTER BANK CUTS BASE RATE
National Westminster Bank Plc said it hascut its base lending rate  0.5 percentage points to 10.5 pcttoday.
    National Westminster said it was responding to generaleasing in money market rates.    Its move followed a signal from the Bank of England earlierthis afternoon that it would endorse a half point cut in thebase rate, a surprise move following its strong signals lastweek that such a move would be premature.    However, since then the pound has continued to gainstrongly.
