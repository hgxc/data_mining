ROYAL BANK/CANADA UPS U.S. BASE RATE
<Royal Bank of Canada> said it israising its U.S. base lending rate by 1/4 pct to 8-1/4 pct,effective tomorrow.    The move is the first change in the bank's U.S. dollar baselending rate since last August, when it lowered the rate 1/2point.    It follows the announcement yesterday of a 1/4 point riseto 7-3/4 pct of two of the largest U.S. banks' prime rates.
