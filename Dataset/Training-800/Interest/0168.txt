MORE U.S. BANKS JOIN IN PRIME RATE CUT
Chemical Bank, the main bank subsidiaryof Chemical New York Corp, and Marine Midland Banks Inc saidthey were raising the prime lending rate to 7-3/4 pct from7-1/2 pct, effective immediately.    In St Louis, Mercantile Bancorp said its Mercantile BankN.A. was also raising its prime rate to 7-3/4 pct from 7-1/2pct, effective immediately.    The changes follow similar cuts yesterday by Citicorp andChase Manhattan.
