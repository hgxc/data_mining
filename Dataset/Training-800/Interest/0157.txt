TREASURY'S BAKER HOPES PRIME RATE RISE TEMPORARY
Treasury Secretary James Baker saidhe hopes yesterday's small increase in two major money centerbanks' prime rate was a temporary phenomenon.    "I hope it was a temporary blip upward," he told a HouseAppropriations subcommittee.    He said the decline in interest rates since PresidentReagan took office remains "one of the significantaccomplishments, in the economic area, of this administration.
