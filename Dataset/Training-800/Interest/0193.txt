ROYAL BANK CANADA LOWERS CREDIT CARD RATE
<Royal Bank of Canada> said it wouldlower its Visa credit card interest rate to 15.9 pct from 18.6pct, effective with the May billing statement.
    The bank said it would retain the credit card user fee of12 dlrs yearly or 15 cts a transaction.    Its move came one day after <Canadian Imperial Bank ofCommerce> lowered its credit card interest rate to the samelevel. The Canadian government last month threatened tolegislate lower credit card rates if financial institutions didnot voluntarily do so.
