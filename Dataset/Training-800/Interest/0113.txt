FED BUYS 500 MLN DLRS OF BILLS FOR CUSTOMER
The Federal Reserve purchased about500 mln dlrs of U.S. Treasury bills for a customer, aspokeswoman said.    She said that the Fed bought bills maturing in June andJuly, and on August 27 and September 10 for regular deliverytomorrow.    Dealers said that Federal funds were trading at 6-1/8 pctwhen the Fed announced the operation.
