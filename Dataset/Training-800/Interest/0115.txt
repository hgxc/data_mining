TREASURY BALANCES AT FED ROSE ON MARCH 23
Treasury balances at the FederalReserve rose on March 23 to 3.332 billion dlrs from 3.062billion dlrs on the previous business day, the Treasury said inits latest budget statement.
    Balances in tax and loan note accounts fell to 15.513billion dlrs from 17.257 billion dlrs on the same respectivedays.
    The Treasury's operating cash balance totaled 18.845billion dlrs on March 23 compared with 20.318 billion dlrs onMarch 20.
