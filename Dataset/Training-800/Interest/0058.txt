U.K. BANKS OFFER FIXED-RATE MORTGAGES AT 10.2 PCT
Two major U.K. Clearing banks announcedthey will offer a total of 800 mln stg in new mortgages at afixed interest rate of 10.2 pct for the first three years,below current bank base lending rates of 10.5 pct.
