CCC ACCEPTS BONUS BID ON BARLEY TO CYRPUS
The Commodity Credit Corporation(CCC) has accepted a bid on an export bonus to cover the saleof 8,000 tonnes of barley to Cyprus, the U.S. AgricultureDepartment said.    The barley is for shipment May 5-20 and the bonus of 39.32dlrs per tonne was made to Harvest States Cooperatives, thedepartment said.
    The bonus will be paid in the form of commodities from theinventory of CCC stocks.    The department said an additional 42,000 tonnes of U.S.barley are still available to Cyprus under the ExportEnhancement Program initiative announced on August 26, 1986. Reuter
