PANAMANIAN WHEAT SHIP STILL GROUNDED OFF SYRIA
The Panamanian bulk carrier Juvena isstill aground outside Tartous, Syria, despite discharging 6,400tons of its 39,000-ton cargo of wheat, and water has enteredthe engine-room due to a crack in the vessel bottom, LloydsShipping Intelligence Service said.    The Juvena, 53,351 tonnes dw, ran aground outside Tartousport basin breakwater on February 25 in heavy weather and roughseas. Reuter
