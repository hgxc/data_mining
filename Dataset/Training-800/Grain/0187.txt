HONDURAS AUTHORIZED TO BUY PL 480 COMMODITIES
Honduras has been authorized to buyabout 75,000 tonnes of U.S. wheat, about 15,000 tonnes of U.S.corn, and about 6,000 tonnes of U.S. tallow under an existingPL 480 agreement, the U.S. Agriculture Department said.    The department said it may buy the wheat, valued at 8.5 mlndlrs, the corn, valued at 1.
5 mln, and the tallow, valued at2.0 mln dlrs, between March 24 and August 31, 1987, and ship itfrom U.S. ports and/or Canadian transshipment points by thisSeptember 30.    The purchase authorizations cover the entire quantityprovided under the agreement, signed March 11.
