AUSTRALIAN WHEAT EXPORTS RISE IN FEBRUARY
Australian wheat shipments rose to1.33 mln tonnes in February from 1.01 mln in January, anAustralian Wheat Board official said.
    February's shipments were down on the 1.54 mln tonnesshipped in February 1986.    Cumulative shipments for the first five months of theOctober/September wheat year were 6.
12 mln tonnes, as against6.54 mln a year earlier, the official said.    The major destinations in February were: China (419,196tonnes), Egypt (301,909), Iraq (142,055), Japan (110,261),South Korea (100,847) and the Soviet Union (100,056 tonnes).
