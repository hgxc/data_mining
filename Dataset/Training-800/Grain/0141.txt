USDA SAYS WHEAT PURCHASED FROM FOREIGN SELLERS
The U.S. Agriculture Department saidprivate U.S. exporters reported purchases from foreign sellersof 120,000 tonnes of wheat for delivery to unknowndestinations.    The wheat, consisting of 60,000 tonnes of hard red winterand 60,000 tones of soft red winter, is for delivery during the1987/88 season, which begins June 1.    A purchase from a foreign seller is a transaction in whicha U.S. exporter contracts to buy U.S.-produced commodities froma foreign firm, the department said.
