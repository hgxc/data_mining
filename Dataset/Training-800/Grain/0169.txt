U.S. SENATOR UNCOMMITTED ON OFFERING 0/92 BILL
Senator Richard Lugar of Indiana,ranking Republican on the U.S. Senate Agriculture Committee,has not decided whether to introduce an administration-backedbill to apply the so-called 0/92 provision to 1988 through 1990grain crops, an aide to the senator said.    The Reagan administration has asked Lugar to offer themeasure, the aide said.    However, a number of farm groups have told Lugar theyoppose the proposal on the grounds it would reopen the 1985farm bill, and the senator has decided to take a second look atthe proposal, the aide said.
    Last week the aide indicated Lugar was planning to offerthe 0/92 measure and a bill to tighten a payment limitationloophole. Reuter
