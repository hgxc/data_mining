MADAGASCAR RICE CROP ESTIMATED HIGHER IN 1987
Madagascar's vital rice crop isestimated at 2,286,000 tonnes of paddy this year, up from2,138,000 in 1986, the Ministry of Agriculture said.    The Trade Ministry said rice imports quadrupled in localcurrency value during the first nine months of last year as thegovernment established a buffer stock of the country's staplefood.
