EC TO MULL NEW DURUM WHEAT INTERVENTION RULES
The European Commission is to considerproposed new higher minimum standards for sales of durum wheatinto intervention stores, European Community sources said.    They said a document drawn up by Commission officialsproposes a reduction in the maximum humidity level to 13 pctfrom 14, an increase in the minimum weight to 78 kilos perhectolitre from 76, a tightening of other technical standardsand introduction of some new ones.
