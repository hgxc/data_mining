U.S. GRAIN CARLOADINGS FALL IN WEEK
U.S. grain carloadings totaled 26,108cars in the week ended February 21, down 2.2 pct from theprevious week but 22.8 pct above the corresponding week a yearago, the Association of American Railroads reported.
    Grain mill product loadings in the week totalled 11,382cars, down 1.8 pct from the previous week but 7.6 pct above thesame week a year earlier, the association said.
