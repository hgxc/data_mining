CCC ACCEPTS EXPORT BONUS BARLEY MALT TO NIGERIA
The Commodity Credit Corporation,CCC, has accepted one bonus offer from an exporter on the saleof 4,400 tonnes of barley malt to Nigeria, the U.S. AgricultureDepartment said.    The department said the bonus awarded was 100.00 dlrs pertonne and was made to Rahr Malting Co and will be paid in theform of commodities from the inventory stocks of the CCC.    The barley malt is scheduled for shipment during April,1987.
    An additional 76,300 tonnes of barley malt are stillavailable to Nigeria under the Export Enhancement Programinitiative announced December 10, 1986, it said. Reuter
