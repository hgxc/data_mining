CHINESE WHEAT CROP THREATENED BY PESTS, DISEASE
China's wheat crop this year is seriouslythreatened by plant pests and diseases, the New China NewsAgency said.    More than 5 mln hectares of wheat-producing land in NorthChina could be affected because relatively warm and dry weatherhad allowed bacteria and insect eggs to survive the winter, theagency added.    China"s Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry andFisheries has called for measures including the timely supplyof pesticides to farmers to combat the threat, it said.
