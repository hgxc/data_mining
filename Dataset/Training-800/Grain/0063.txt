CCC ACCEPTS BONUS BID ON SEMOLINA TO EGYPT
The Commodity Credit Corporation,CCC, has accepted a bid for an export bonus to cover a sale of2,000 tonnes of semolina to Egypt, the U.S. AgricultureDepartment said.    The department said the bonus was 233.91 dlrs per tonne andwas made to International Multifoods Corp.
 The bonus will bepaid in the form of commodities from CCC stocks.    The semolina is for shipment during June 1987, it said.    An additional 13,000 tonnes of semolina are still availableto Egypt under the Export Enhancement Program initiativeannounced on August 6, 1986, it said.
