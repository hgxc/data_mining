TAIWAN'S FIRST QUARTER MAIZE IMPORTS SEEN RISING
Taiwan's maize import commitments areexpected to rise to 970,000 tonnes in the first four months of1987 from 870,000 tonnes a year earlier, a spokesman for theJoint Committee of Maize Importers told Reuters.    He said more than 75 pct of the imports come from the U.S.And the rest from South Africa.    The maize import target for calendar 1987 is set at wellover 3.
