CANADA-EGYPT WHEAT NEGOTIATIONS TO CONTINUE
Canadian and Egyptian wheat negotiatorsfailed to conclude an agreement on Canadian wheat exports toEgypt during talks last week, but the Canadian team will returnto Cairo for further negotiations, Canadian embassy officialssaid.    An embassy official declined to identify which issuesremained to be resolved and when the talks would resume.    In a five-year protocol signed in 1985, Cairo agreed topurchase 500,000 tonnes of Canadian wheat a year.
