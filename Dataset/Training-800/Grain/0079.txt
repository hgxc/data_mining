BRAZIL SUSPENDS IMPORT OF 500,000 TONNES MAIZE
Brazil has suspended the importationof 500,000 tonnes of maize ordered last year because of theexcellent domestic maize harvest expected this year,Agriculture Minister Iris Resende said.    The Agriculture Ministry expects a record maize crop of27.7 mln tonnes, a 36 pct increase on last year's crop of 20.
3mln tonnes.    Brazil's total grain crop is expected to be 65.3 mlntonnes.
 "This is a record in the history of Brazilianagriculture," a ministry spokesman said.    Resende announced suspension of the maize imports at a newsconference in Brasilia yesterday.    The ministry spokesman said he had no other details on themaize transaction.
