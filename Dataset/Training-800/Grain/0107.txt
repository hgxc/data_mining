CCC WHEAT CREDIT GUARANTEES FOR NORTH YEMEN
The Commodity Credit Corporation,CCC, has authorized 25.0 mln dlrs in credit guarantees to NorthYemen to cover purchases of U.S. wheat under the IntermediateExport Credit Guarantee Program (GSM-103), the U.S. AgricultureDepartment said.    Under the program credit terms extended must be in excessof three yeras, but not more than seven years.
    All sales under the line must be registered and exportscompleted by September 30, 1987, the department said. Reuter
